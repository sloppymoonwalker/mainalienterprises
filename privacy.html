Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Last Updated: January 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Mainali Software Enterprises LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates mobile applications, websites, and digital services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use any of our applications or services (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.

2. Applications Covered

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications and services operated by Mainali Software Enterprises LLC, including but not limited to:

  • HomeChata
  • IdeaRiz
  • Any future applications we develop and publish

Individual applications may have supplementary privacy terms specific to their functionality, which will be provided within the respective application.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our Services, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Content you create, upload, or share within our applications
  • Communications you send to us (support requests, feedback)

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage data (features used, interactions, session duration)
  • IP address
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • General location data (city/region level, not precise location unless you grant permission)

3.3 Location Data

Some of our applications may request access to your device's location. Location access is only used when you explicitly enable it for specific features. We do not continuously track your location in the background. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

3.4 Payment Information

We do not directly collect or store your payment card information. All payments are processed through third-party payment processors (Apple App Store, Google Play Store). These processors handle your payment information according to their own privacy policies.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Process transactions and manage your account
  • Send you important account notifications and updates
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services and APIs

Our applications may use third-party services and APIs to provide functionality. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies. Third-party services we may use include:

  • Google Maps Platform — For location-based services, maps, and place information
  • Supabase — For user authentication and data storage
  • RevenueCat — For purchase management and subscription tracking
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — For payment processing and app distribution
  • Expo — For push notifications and app updates
  • Analytics providers — For app usage analytics and crash reporting

Third-Party Data Disclaimer: Data and information provided through third-party APIs (including but not limited to location data, place information, ratings, and other content) are provided "as is" by their respective sources. We do not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of third-party data. We are not responsible for errors, omissions, or inaccuracies in data provided by third-party services.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by our infrastructure partners. We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of passwords (hashed, never stored in plain text)
  • Secure authentication tokens
  • Access controls and monitoring

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Usage data and analytics may be retained in anonymized form for longer periods to help us improve our Services.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal information
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of certain data collection and marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@mainalienterprises.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for users who are at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to delete such information.

If you are between 13 and 18, you must have parental or guardian consent to use our Services.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your information)
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@mainalienterprises.com.

11. International Users

Our Services are operated from the United States. If you are accessing our Services from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our Services, you consent to this transfer and processing.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through our applications or by email. Your continued use of our Services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
